Health insurance is a type of insurance coverage that pays for medical and surgical expenses incurred by the insured. It is designed to protect individuals and families from high medical costs. Having health insurance is important because it can help cover the cost of expensive medical treatments, including hospital stays, surgeries, and prescription drugs. It can also help protect individuals and families from financial ruin in the event of a serious illness or injury. The benefits of health insurance include access to necessary medical care, protection from high medical costs, and financial security. Working with a reputed health insurance advisor like Harish Shukla can help individuals and families navigate the process of selecting and purchasing the right health insurance plan for their needs.
